Most clients have no idea what they want.

It's not that they're ignorant. In fact, they're very smart about their own business. They usually have no idea as to what they want on the website and how the whole thing works. So, it's really your job to ask questions.

Ask the right questions

the right questions to help the client figure out what it is that they want and kind of communicate that back to you. A great way of doing it is if you bring some website examples of similar businesses that already have websites and have the client react to those websites.
